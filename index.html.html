Biography

The Musical Journey

Shruthi Shankar Kumar is a rising artiste in the Carnatic music circuit, known for her authenticity, classicism, and technical finesse. Her music reflects a strong adherence to tradition while offering depth in manodharma, with concerts that feature a thoughtfully curated repertoire spanning a wide range of composers.

Shruthi is an A Grade artiste of All India Radio and has been a regular performer during the prestigious Margazhi music season in Chennai since 2014, presenting concerts at leading sabhas including The Music Academy, Sri Parthasarathy Swami Sabha, Narada Gana Sabha and Brahma Gana Sabha, among others.

She has undergone over two decades of rigorous training in Carnatic music and is currently under the advanced tutelage of Vidwan Delhi Sri P. Sunder Rajan for over a decade. She was previously trained by Palghat Sri T. R. Rajaram, son of the legendary mridangam maestro Palghat Sri Mani Iyer, grounding her firmly in a rich musical lineage.
